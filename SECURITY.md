# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you believe you have discovered a security vulnerability, please report it to us through [GitHub Security Advisories](https://github.com/TaimoorSiddiquiOfficial/HopCode/security/advisories/new).

> **Note:** This channel is strictly for reporting security-related issues. Non-security vulnerabilities or general bug reports should be filed as [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/TaimoorSiddiquiOfficial/HopCode/issues) instead.

We sincerely appreciate your responsible disclosure and your contribution to helping us keep our project secure.
